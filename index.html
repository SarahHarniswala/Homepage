About



I am a PhD Candidate researching Public Policy at the Ashank Desai Centre for Policy Studies at IIT Bombay.
My research delves into the field of structural inequalities with a focus on gender and sexuality.

Academic Projects & Papers

Presented a paper on Ambivalent Recognition of Queers under Authoritarian Populism submitted to the Panel: Authoritarianism across space, time, and scale at the European Consortium for Political Research’s (ECPR) Joint Sessions of Workshops, April 2022, The University of Edinburgh.

The same paper (with some modification) is under consideration for the Journal of Gender Studies’s Special Issue—Gender: Ambivalent In_Visibilities.

MA Public Administration Project—Analyzing the Policy of Corporate Social Responsibility in India through the Case Study of SAIL and Tata Steel.

Presented a research paper titled The Bhadralok Culture in West Bengal at a Students' Seminar Culture, Identity and Multiculturalism in March 2016 at Jamia Millia Islamia University, New Delhi.

Presented a research paper titled Implementation of NRHM in UP: Manifestation of Corruption and Deterioration of Ethics at an International Seminar on State Politics and Public Administration in UP in June 2016 at Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi.
